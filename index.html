Welcome to Brecon Badminton Club based in Swindon, Wiltshire. We are a friendly group of people from all backgrounds with a passion for badminton. We welcome experienced club players to join us for fun and competitive play at an intermediate to advanced level. We play with feather shuttlecocks. For those who wish to play competitively Brecon BC has a Mixed team in Division 1, a Ladies team in Division 1 and 2 Men's teams in Divisions 2 & 3 of the Swindon & District Badminton league. Club nights are on Tuesday (4 courts) & Friday (3 courts). Home match night is Friday. We play all year round with short breaks at Christmas and Easter. Contact us using the form below and come and try the club.
